  • retnia
    Ocular oximetry tools

    Retnia has developed a patented, non-invasive device to accurately measure the concentration of oxygen in the human eye. The technology enables researchers and eye-care professionals to gain insight into pathophysiological and metabolic processes. It offers applications for screening, diagnosing, monitoring, and managing the care of diseases such as glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy and age-related macular degeneration.

    Mentum has participated in the data engineering process, from data gathering to the data analysis software.
